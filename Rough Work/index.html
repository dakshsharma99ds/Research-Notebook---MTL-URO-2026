Matter to Life: Research-Inspired MVPs

Interactive demos inspired by Zwicker, Loos, and Dietz labs

Lab 1: Self-Organization & Pattern Formation (Zwicker)

Notes / Summary:

  • Studied self-organization of particles from random motion.
  • Observed how interactions lead to clustering and emergent patterns.
  • Implemented simple particle simulation to visualize this process.

Lab 2: Stochastic Processes in Cells (Loos)

Notes / Summary:

  • Studied randomness in cell division and death.
  • Used stochastic simulations to observe population dynamics.
  • Learned about how probabilities affect growth patterns.

Lab 3: DNA Origami & Nanomachines (Dietz)

Notes / Summary:

  • Studied DNA folding for nanoscale machines.
  • Implemented a simple folding animation to visualize DNA origami.
  • Learned about structure-function relationships at nanoscale.
